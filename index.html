Why This Matters

The workforce shortage isn't just a numberâ€”it directly impacts patient outcomes and the stability of our healthcare system.

Delayed Diagnosis

Fewer professionals mean longer queues for critical diagnostic imaging, delaying life-saving treatments for cancer and acute conditions.

Treatment Backlogs

Radiation therapy units cannot operate at full capacity without qualified staff, leading to extended wait times for cancer patients.

Staff Burnout

Existing staff are under immense pressure, leading to high turnover rates and further destabilizing the essential healthcare workforce.
